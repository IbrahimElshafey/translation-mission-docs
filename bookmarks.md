* [GLiNER](https://github.com/urchade/GLiNER)
  * GLiNER is a framework for training and deploying small Named Entity Recognition (NER) models with zero-shot capabilities. In addition to tradition NER,
     it also supports joint entity and relation extraction. GLiNER is fine-tunable, optimized to run on CPUs and consumer hardware,
     and has performance competitive with LLMs several times its size, like ChatGPT and UniNER.
